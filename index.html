Visit Ekiti, Nigeria

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Ekiti.

Top three activities to do at Ekiti

Attend a show or a concert

The Lagos Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park

The 6 Picnic-Perfect Park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Onikan Stadium

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
